LENS PRESCRIPTION CALCULATOR

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Remove your glasses.
  • Get close to the screen until you see the word 'HELLO' below sharply.
  • Slowly move your head back until 'HELLO' becomes blurred (to edge of blur).
  • Record the exact distance in centimeters from your eyes to the screen. ( using ruler/measuring tape )
  • If you don't know your prescription, you can leave it empty.

Note: This is just a project and not intended for medical use.
